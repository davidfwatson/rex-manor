## Selection Criteria

- **Benefit**: How many people expected to attend/benefit
- **Involvement**: How many people help organize
- **Program Design**: Is the event well-planned

## Permits Needed

- **Block Party Permit**: Required for events on city streets. Contact Police Dept at 650-903-6344.
- **Park Permit** (if applicable): Contact Recreation Division at 650-903-6331 or www.MountainView.gov/Reservations

## Reimbursement Rules

- Funds are disbursed on a **quarterly reimbursement basis** (you pay first, get reimbursed)
- Only out-of-pocket expenses are eligible
- Checks issued to event team leader
- Must submit dated receipts + event description via the online reimbursement form
- Restaurant/catered food: max $10/person, delivery max $50
- No alcohol

## Post-Event Requirements

- Written activity/event summary documenting achievements
- May be asked to present to the CNC

## Eligible Activities (for a block party/BBQ)

- Block parties/picnics
- Live music
- Food (with per-person caps)
- Youth events
- Neighborhood scavenger hunts
- Recruitment events

## Ineligible Expenditures

- Alcohol
- Political activities
- Equipment primarily for individual use (e.g., portable grills)
- HOA-specific events
