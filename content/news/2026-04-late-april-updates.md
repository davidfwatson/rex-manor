Hello neighbors,

As April winds down, there are still plenty of fantastic opportunities to connect with our vibrant community and celebrate the unique character of Mountain View. It is inspiring to see how our city continues to thrive and evolve, bringing new cultural and technological experiences to our doorstep.

### Honoring Tech Pioneers

Tonight, April 25, the [Computer History Museum](https://computerhistory.org/) is hosting their prestigious **2026 Fellow Awards**. This event honors the visionaries who have profoundly shaped our digital world, including the creators of the first successful handheld computers and early smartphones, the inventor of FM audio synthesis, and the pioneer of internet search and digital preservation. It is a powerful reminder of how fortunate we are to live in a city at the heart of such incredible, world-changing innovation!

### Arts and Entertainment Downtown

If you are looking for an uplifting and powerful theatrical experience, the acclaimed musical **"Come From Away"** is running at the [Mountain View Center for the Performing Arts (MVCPA)](https://mvcpa.com/) through May 10. It’s a wonderful opportunity to support the arts right here in our vibrant downtown. Speaking of downtown, as Mountain View continues to grow, it’s exciting to see new businesses and housing opportunities enriching our city’s character. Embracing this positive growth ensures our community remains a welcoming and dynamic place for everyone.

### Neighborhood Life

Looking ahead to summer, we are already starting to plan for our **Rex Manor Annual Block Party**! Mark your calendars for Sunday, June 7, 2026. This is always a highlight of the year, bringing our community together for food, fun, and great conversation. If you would like to help with organizing or have any ideas for this year’s event, please reach out. We rely on volunteers to make these gatherings a success, and it’s a wonderful way to meet your neighbors.

Enjoy the rest of your April!

— David Watson
