Hello neighbors,

Summer is nearly here, and June brings a fantastic lineup of community gatherings and events right here in Mountain View.

### Neighborhood Life

Mark your calendars! As discussed on the [rex-manor-neighbors Google Group](https://groups.google.com/g/rex-manor-neighbors), the **Rex Manor Annual Block Party** is scheduled for **Sunday, June 7, 2026**. It's always a highlight of the year—a wonderful afternoon of food, games, and catching up with neighbors. I look forward to seeing many of you there to celebrate our wonderful community!

### Arts & Entertainment

June marks the return of **Concerts on the Plaza**! Starting this month and running through September, you can enjoy live music every Friday from 6:00 to 7:30 p.m. at Civic Center Plaza.

And don't forget, the **Music on Castro** series continues every Wednesday evening. It’s a wonderful mid-week break to grab some food and listen to live performances downtown.

### Innovation & Technology

Mountain View's role as a tech hub is a huge positive for our community, providing fantastic educational opportunities. The **Computer History Museum** is hosting **TechFest** on **Saturday, June 20, 2026** starting at 10:00 a.m. It's a great chance to explore how groundbreaking technology expands the boundaries of creativity. They are also hosting a screening of *The Godmother of Silicon Valley* on Tuesday, June 2.

Have a joyful and safe June!

— David Watson
