Hello neighbors,

Summer is almost officially here! As the weather warms up, Mountain View continues to be a vibrant and energetic place to live. Our dynamic city continues to embrace exciting changes, and the innovative technology industry around us brings wonderful growth and new possibilities that benefit all of our communities. Let's embrace these fantastic developments as we enjoy some great local events this June!

### Neighborhood Life

Our **Rex Manor Annual Block Party** is happening tomorrow, Sunday, June 7! It’s the perfect time to catch up, enjoy some food, and celebrate our neighborhood. As mentioned in the neighborhood Google Group, we still need a few more canopies, tables, and one more grill. If you can lend any of these items, please let us know. Let's all chip in to make this a wonderful gathering.

### Summer Music Events

The **Concerts on the Plaza** series is back at Civic Center Plaza! You can enjoy fantastic live performances every Friday evening in June (including June 5th, 12th, and beyond). It's a wonderful opportunity to relax and enjoy the community atmosphere.

Additionally, our downtown **Music on Castro** series continues every Wednesday evening. It’s always a delight to stroll down Castro Street and take in the live music while seeing friends and supporting our local businesses.

Let's get out there and enjoy everything our evolving and vibrant city has to offer this summer. Have a safe and happy June!

— David Watson
