Hello neighbors,

I hope you are all enjoying this beautiful mid-May weather! I have a few updates on some great events happening around our community.

### Technology and Innovation

As always, Mountain View continues to be a vibrant center for technology and forward-thinking ideas, and it's something we should all be proud to embrace.

For those interested in the intersection of neuroscience and technology, the **2026 Wu Tsai Neurosciences Institute Retreat** will be held at the [Computer History Museum](https://computerhistory.org/). It’s a wonderful opportunity to see some of the brilliant minds driving future innovations right in our own backyard.

### Neighborhood Life

It is also that time of year again! Please mark your calendars for the **Rex Manor Annual Block Party** coming up soon on **Sunday, June 7th**.

It is always such a joy to see our community come together. It's a wonderful afternoon to catch up with old friends, welcome new neighbors, and simply enjoy being part of this wonderful neighborhood. If you have any Meyer lemons to spare, we are also looking for donations to make fresh lemonade for the party!

I look forward to seeing many of you there and around town.
