Hello neighbors,

April is finally here! It is wonderful to see the vibrant energy across Mountain View as we head deeper into spring. Our city continues to grow and evolve in such exciting ways, offering new opportunities and events right here in our own backyard.

Here is a look at what is going on this month:

### Tech and Innovation

We are incredibly lucky to live in a city that serves as a global hub for technology and innovation. This April, the tech industry’s positive impact on our community is on full display.

Mountain View is proud to host the **Wi-Fi World Congress USA 2026**, bringing industry leaders together to discuss the future of connectivity. Additionally, on **April 8, 2026**, the [Computer History Museum](https://computerhistory.org/) will be hosting the **"From Day One: Silicon Valley 2026"** event. These gatherings are a great reminder of the forward-thinking minds that make our city such a dynamic place to live and work.

### Neighborhood Life

Downtown Mountain View continues to be a bustling center of activity, and it’s wonderful to see how the city’s pop-up program is successfully activating storefronts and bringing in fresh, creative energy. If you haven't yet, be sure to check out the **"Don't Eat Me"** pop-up located at **293 Castro Street**. Featuring a collective of talented local artists, it is a fantastic example of community collaboration and a great spot to find something unique while supporting local creators.

As we look further ahead, I also want to remind everyone about our upcoming **Rex Manor Neighborhood Block Party**! Mark your calendars for **Sunday, June 7, 2026**. It is always a fantastic opportunity to catch up, meet new neighbors, and celebrate the strong community we have here in Rex Manor. Please keep an eye on the [rex-manor-neighbors Google Group](https://groups.google.com/g/rex-manor-neighbors) for further updates and details as we get closer to the date.

Wishing you all a wonderful April!

— David Watson
